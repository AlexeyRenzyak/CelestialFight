Celestial Fight

How to start the game:
Open the Celestial Fight folder in terminal or IDE and launch the CelestialFight.py script

Current Features:
-Inertia-based player movement
-Shooting and asteroid damage mechanic
-Death after collision with an asteroid or going out of bounds
-Upgrade mechanics (Extra Life, Speed, Rate of Fire, Dampening, Difficulty Decrease)
-Three types of asteroid enemies: ordinary, fast and small, explosive
-Gradual difficulty increase
-Sound toggle
-In-game pause
